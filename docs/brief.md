# Product Brief: 물식물

## Problem (1 sentence)
All my plants die because I have no watering reminders, and existing apps are expensive or ad-ridden.

## Solution (1 sentence)
A downloadable app that runs locally with a local database — no internet required.

## First customers
Myself.

## Launch price
Free.

## Delivery date
April 6, 2026.

## 3 MVP Features

### 1. Add my plants (offline)
I can register plants I own (photo, name, date acquired) while completely offline.
**Why:** Track all my plants in one place.

### 2. Plant care information
View plant details: water consumption, last watered date, light needs, toxicity to pets, and care tips.
**Why:** I want useful information about my plants.

### 3. Watering notifications
Receive push notifications whenever a plant needs watering.
**Why:** I forget to water my plants too often.

## What the MVP does NOT include
- **Paid features** — this app is for me. Maybe later if I want to publish on the App Store or Play Store.
- **Social/community features** — friend lists, shared gardens, etc. Not needed now.
